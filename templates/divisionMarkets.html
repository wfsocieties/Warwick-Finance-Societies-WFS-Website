Markets

Providing a platform for members to engage with financial markets in a discursive and informal setting.


Aaron Bird
Xiangtong Liu

We tailor our sessions for anyone with an interest in the financial markets. Whether you're a seasoned investor or just starting out, we hope you find our content engaging. We Offer:

  • Interactive Market Analysis: We host weekly discussions where students explore current events and trends shaping the markets.
  • Technical Sessions: These cover a range of topics including different asset classes, stock pitches, and financial analysis. We also organise events to equip members with essential skills like coding and Excel.
  • Industry Connections: We connect students with professionals through guest speakers from areas such as sales and trading, asset management, and more. We also work with sponsors to host networking events.
  • Engaging Competitions: We run market-based competitions that are both educational and fun alongside technical quizzes to help prepare members for S&T interviews.
