Alternatives

Delve into research, portfolios and strategies across asset classes.


Taksh Shrivastra
Thomas John

The Alternatives Division leads discussions on various topics, from venture capital to hedge funds. We invite speakers with years of experience to provide valuable insights, providing our members the chance to learn directly from professionals who play pivotal roles in the industry. At our events, you'll find us pitching companies as growth and leveraged buyout targets, dissecting trends in alternative asset classes and organising Dragons' Den events that challenge participants to think critically and creatively. We also host technical sessions that would equip you with skills essential on the the buy-side. So if you're thinking to work as a private equity or investment analyst, you know where to come. One of our hallmark events is the Start-up Pitch Competition we host in collaboration with NatWest and experienced venture capitalists - we certainly hope to see you there!
