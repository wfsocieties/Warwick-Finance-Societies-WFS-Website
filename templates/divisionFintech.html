Fintech

The Fintech Division empowers students to explore and innovate at the intersection of finance and technology, preparing them to lead in a rapidly evolving digital economy.


Noah Morgan-Doyle
Iyin Olubajo

Welcome to Warwick Fintech! Our division brings together and allows members to learn about the cutting-edge innovations and challenges shaping the future of financial technology. From the expanding world of Digital Currencies to the competitive landscape of digitalized banking, Fintech is currently the fastest-paced area of Finance, with some truly revolutionary innovations happening monthly. We host events of all different types which dive into diverse topics such as robo-investing, multinational fraud or the latest AI use in finance. Our biggest event is the WFS Fintech Week with previous world-renowned speakers such as Global Head of Wise, Forbes 30 under 30 entrepreneurs and partners from a leading specialized Venture Capitalist fund. This year, we plan to host Fintech's inaugural hackathon. A weekend-long event with prizes, exclusive opportunities and informative sessions at the end of Fintech week. Whether it's the talks, the technical sessions or the speakers, every event from Fintech is guaranteed to be interesting and at the frontier of the field. Stay tuned for more updates!
