WFS Resources

At the Warwick Finance Societies (WFS), we are dedicated to providing our members with a wealth of resources to support their career aspirations in the finance industry. One of the ways we achieve this is by sharing valuable materials, such as slides from our events, workshops, and guest speaker sessions. These resources are designed to enhance your understanding of various finance topics, from investment banking to private equity, and to equip you with the knowledge needed to excel in competitive roles. By making these materials accessible, we aim to foster a collaborative learning environment where members can gain insights from real professionals and apply their learning to real-world scenarios. Together, we are committed to ensuring that every member has the tools and opportunities necessary to thrive in their finance careers.
